Turbine Services – Automated Morning Report 2025

Customers are informed each morning about that day’s planned service operations via a report sent by the management team. This was previously done manually, taking around 30 minutes per person each day. I built a script using Playwright to scrape the relevant service data from Salesforce and turbine status data from SCADA, navigating pages and waiting for dynamic content to load before extracting the data and compiling it into the final report.

Time saving potential: ~960 hours/year (8 persons × 30 min × 20 days × 12 months)

Generator Rental Comparison Tool 2025

During grid outages, wind turbine blades cannot be yawed (nacelle turned) towards the wind. If the wind blows from the wrong angle, blades can start to vibrate and become damaged. I built a price and specification comparison site pulling data from Cramo and Ramirent websites, making it easier for technicians to find and rent the right generator to power the yaw motors.
